Process
LeanUX & Collaborative design thinking

While I am skilled in a variety of traditional UX methods, I specialize in lean UX methods such as guerilla (or just plain quick and simple) testing, design session moderation, rapid prototyping, sketching and building collaborative relationship with project partners.

The biggest challenges I face and continue to find ways to overcome are wrangling old systems in to polished, usable interfaces. I seek to implement creative solutions on top of inflexible databases and legacy systems, hopefully rippling change back to the core.
